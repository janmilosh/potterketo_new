Why Keto?

The Ketogenic Diet has been proven an effective treatment for Epilepsy. More recent research suggests that it may also be effective in treating other conditions such as metabolic disease, Alzheimer's, autism, Lou Gehrig's Disease (ALS), Parkinson's, and migrane headaches.
